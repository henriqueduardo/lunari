LUNARI is
Turkish boutique hotel located on the seaside, surrounded by beautiful nature and landscapes. The hotel was built on the site of and old castle, built in the 18th century to protect against enemy invasions

Over the years, Lunari has become not only a favorite place for tourists to relax but also a popular venue for weddings, corporate events, and other celebrations
